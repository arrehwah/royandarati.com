samasta, a microfinance company headquartered in bangalore,
provides micro credit to the urban and rural poor of india.
the meaning of samasta is ‘all-encompassing’ which reflects
their business. the symbol forms a simple geometric fractal that
can be used as a pattern to be repeated and combined.
the concentric human circles represent faith in people,
community and society working together for the greater good.
