started in 2003 by shabnam virmani, a documentary filmmaker
and artist-in-residence at srishti school of art, design and technology,
‘the kabir project’ brings together the experiences of a series of journeys
in quest of this 15th century mystic poet in our contemporary world.
it consists of 4 documentary films, 2 folk music videos and 10 music cds
accompanied by books of the poetry in translation.

we designed a 72-page book accompanying the double cd ‘kabir in malwa’.
the lyrics, along with english translations, notes and anecdotes by
shabnam virmani, are interwoven with imagery from the poetry.  we illustrated
the poetry with its myriad metaphors and meanings, doing drawings,
shooting textures and long exposures of the moon as well as using images
from our bank of photographs combined with some footage from the films.
we spent a year on this project and it has been an exciting journey.

we are currently designing the kabir project website (april 2009).
