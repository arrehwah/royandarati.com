we are a graphic design studio based in bangalore, india. we love collaborations,
change and lightness.

we specialise in print and illustration - branding and identity, posters, books
and publications, packaging, information graphics, murals, invitations, t-shirts...
we also enjoy working with spaces, both physical spaces and the web,
adapting our design process to different requirements and across media,
bringing passion, commitment and honesty to everything we do.

we returned to bangalore to set up our own studio in 2007, combining our
experiences and strengths.  roy illustrates and takes photographs and arati learns
indian classical music and dance.  we like to seek and experience the simplicity
of life as it fuels our ability to create.  we find peace in doing meaningful work
and love collaborations that create change.

roy >>
roy was brought up in bangalore, did a bachelors in businesss management and realised he wanted to do graphic design. he joined srishti to do a four-year program. he took a year off from srishti to work with tsk design, Bangalore. after graduating he freelanced for a year, till he got an offer to work in an advertising agency in sri lanka and used it as an opportunity to get out of his comfort zone and experience a whole new life. he moved to colombo where he worked as creative director for ogilvy international before returning to bangalore to set up this design studio in 2007. His daily passions include sketching, illustrating, nature, music, dance, yoga and meditation.
arati >>
arati lived in the north and south of india as a child, till her parents decided to settle in hyderabad. after finishing high school there, she ran as fast as she could from hyderabad, joined srishti in bangalore and started to find herself. after graduating, she moved to chennai to work with 99 and 1 design for two years, then got married to roy and moved to sri lanka. she worked as an art director for an advertising agency but soon realised it was not for her. so she freelanced from home, till they moved back to bangalore and set up this design studio together. her daily passions revolve around animals, nature, space, cooking and feeding people and studying hindustani vocals and bharatnatyam dance.
