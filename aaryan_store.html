aaryan was an indian store in colombo, sri lanka, that retailed indian textiles,
handicrafts, art and furniture.  the store was 2 years old in 2006 when the owners
came to us for a re-branding exercise.  we located a new store space and
redesigned everything - from their logo, stationery, packaging, publicity material,
advertising and memorabilia, to their product display, lighting and store interiors.
