solutions for print and media production.  this package was designed to be
sent out to their clients and team members as a new year gift.  it contained
a set of 5 blank greeting cards with envelopes, and a calendar that
had to be assembled.  this was part of a re-branding exercise designed
and executed in 2004 by 99&1 design, chennai, where arati was then working.
