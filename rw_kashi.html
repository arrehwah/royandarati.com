kashi art gallery, located in fort cochin, celebrated their 10th anniversary
with an exhibition titled ‘ten light years’.  the show featured works by 38 artists
who had exhibited at the gallery over the previous decade.  we designed
a catalogue for the exhibition inspired by the meaning of kashi which is ‘light’.
the cover was laser cut to allow light to pass through it when opened.
we’ve used touches of silver foil inside to reflect light.
